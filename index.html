OUR STORY

Born From Necessity, Built With Purpose

Founded in 2015 after witnessing catastrophic battery fires in early electric vehicles, Elven Technologies set out to create materials that could withstand extreme thermal runaway events without compromising weight or performance.

Today, our team of 120 material scientists, engineers, and safety experts develop cutting-edge solutions trusted by leading automotive, aerospace, and defense organizations worldwide.
